hi, i'm bruno :3

I’m a 20-year-old graphic designer, animator, and motion graphic artist originally from Mexico City, now based in Chicago. Currently, I’m in my third year at The School of The Art Institute of Chicago (SAIC), where I’m focused on visual communications.

I've been immersed in graphic design over the last few years, and I've been learning everything I can to get better since then. I love making dynamic, unique work that challenges me to grow my skillset so I can make cooler projects with the knowledge to back them up and provide real value with my solutions.

Outside of school, I do freelance projects mainly for people in different dance music scenes across the country. I love hardcore dance music, and I DJ at raves and make songs in my free time. I also love video games and anime, and they're a big source of inspiration for my work- I'm currently watching DanDaDan and fire force, and playing Dragonball Sparking! Zero.

If you're interested in collaborating on a project or have any inquiries, feel free to reach out via email at bruno.mrtz.silva@gmail.com or text me at (281)-660-0218 . I’m always excited to take on new opportunities and work on creative projects across all fields!
