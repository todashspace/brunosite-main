my_orange_heart: project overview

freelance work, February 2023

For this project, I was approached to make a brand identity, promotional material, social media posts, and edit full length videos with 3D and 2D animated elements for a radio show featuring DJ's playing underground dance music. I wanted to give it a sleek, colorful, bubbly feel to emphasize the kind of music and artists that would be featured on the program.

Below are intro and outro screens for the full recorded sets on youtube, as well as a short promotional video posted on social media- I made all the "meowballz" in blender, and then brought them into a composition in after effects where I animated all the parts individually.
