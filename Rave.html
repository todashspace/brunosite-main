rave_til_u_die: project overview

beginning graphic design, spring 2024

For this project, I was to find an article of my choosing from the internet and make an interpretation of the text in any way I saw fit. I chose an article on the history of hardcore dance music, both because I love it and beacause I knew it would be a chance to make something colorful and eye catching. To begin, I did a type test to try to find fonts which had that futuristic, energetic, 90's energy I was looking for.

initial concepts

After that, I scoured internet archives looking for genunine pictures from 90's raves. In particular, Wolfgang Tillmans and Tristan O'Neill were two photographers that had the perfect combination of color, spunk, and energy that I was looking for. I gave each page a dedicated color, and only put pictures matching that on the spreads, while also making custom background elements that would match the playful aesthetic.
