natural_ice: project overview

personal project, winter 2024

This series is 3 concepts for rebranding of Natural Ice, one of my favorite beers.

This was a project to push myself on product design, branding, advertisements, and coherent visual identities. I treated it as if it was real work.

current brand identity

>the problem

natty ice is severely underrated, providing cheap beer to parties and special occasions reliably. oftentimes it's in the back of the cooler, or in some dark corner. i wanted it to pop out even in these places, and to change its image for the better so people want to try this often overlooked beer.

>the solution

redesign natural ice for a new audience, captivating their attention through a completely new identity meant to elevate public outlook. design with common placement in stores in mind as well as past history of the product.

initial ideas

>refining current design

this first logo was made to take what's there and not stray too far in feel, but give the design a much needed update for the 2020's.

>evolving the idea

go full 2000's ps2 ad on it- bring the cold and party aspects associated with natty ice to the forefront.

>complete reimagining

throw everything out the window. appeal to a completely new audience while keeping it believable.

idea 1: refine

this idea is the closest in feel to the current design. However it's a refinement on what's already there, refining natty's image with a modern touch while reintroducing it to a newer audience.

fonts used: over there regular, lexend exa

intended themes: refinement, crispness, easily relatable

idea 2: evolve

this iteration focuses on natty being perceived as a party beer. taking this idea and cranking it to 1000, we can completely evolve the feel to invoke the feeling of these often surreal experiences at parties.

fonts used: auurix regular, DIN condensed

intended themes: surreal, physical, cold

idea 3: wildcard

this iteration is all about the social aspect of drinking- focusing partiuclarly on the merriment and deep fulfillment one gets from having a good time with friends and loved ones. the colors and style are all vibrant, and upbeat.

fonts used: astral delight upright black, cambridge market regular

intended themes: friendship, optimism, love
