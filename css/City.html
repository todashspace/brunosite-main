city_scapes: project overview

personal project, August 2022-February 2023

This was an early project when I was starting to get into graphic design and 3D graphics. I included it to show both where I started from, as well as to pay homage due to my style today feeling like an evolution of this project.I made this when I first arrived in Chicago, and it was a good way to both explore the city and to start my journey into design. I went out to find striking compositions in the skyline, and added 3D elements made in blender on top.

I wanted to represent the brand new feeling of energy I had in the city, and the way that life here seemed to have an inheren, optimistic energy to it. I made these feelings manifest through representing them with the shapes and figures I added to the pictures.
