bioluminescent_algae: project overview

beginning graphic design, spring 2024

This project was an infographic based on several science-based topics chosen by our teacher that we could select from. I chose to make it on bioluminescent algae, honestly just because I love the ocean and bioluminescence is one of the coolest things in the world to me. I started out with some type tests, looking for fonts that had an organic, informative feel.

initial concepts

From here, I did extensive research on the actual scientific process and began working on the illustrative elements, making pictures in illustrator for the specific species I would be talking about. The background, as well as all the illustrations on the final version were made by myself.

main illustrations

I then combined the illustrations with the information, trying to find a layout that felt like something I would find interesting to look at while I was bored in class. My initial versions orignally felt too sparce with the first iteration, then too cramped with the second. So, I extended the project to a vertical layout and made the information take up a visually pleasing amount of space in the final version.
